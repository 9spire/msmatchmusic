Biography

Moving bodies & minds is what she does best.

Ms Match brings an obsession-level focus to selecting, combining, and creating the music that keeps her and the crowd moving. Behind the decks, Ms Match channels the universal joy of losing oneself on the dance floor, in that place beyond conscious thought.

Background

Based in Portland, Oregon, Ms Match has played her signature style of vocal-driven, hip-hop influenced breakbeat, tech house, and bass house at most electronic festivals and venues in the area.

Expanding on skills and taking her unique brand of musical experience to new audiences and locations are her primary goals as a DJ. Ms Match is also applying the same passion and discipline to producing, with an EP in sight for 2022.
